Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Expand digital payment options to rural and underserved communities using mobile technology and low-cost smartphones.

Sustainability

Bangladesh has significant solar potential, especially in its rural areas. Expanding solar power, especially solar home systems (SHS).

Infrastructure

Expanding and upgrading rural and urban road networks to reduce traffic congestion and improve accessibility, particularly in less-developed areas.

Expowerment

Expanding access to banking, digital payments, microfinance, and savings accounts for marginalized groups, especially women, rural populations, and small entrepreneurs.
